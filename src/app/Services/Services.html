Seekerfit

Seekerfit is an online job portal accessible from your mobile phone or desktop. Seekerfit's goal is to allow applicants apply for jobs quickly and easily regardless of their location.
Employers get to post vacancies on the platform, review applicants, schedule meetings and have a face-to-face interview.
